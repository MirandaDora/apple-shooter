Your Aim:

 Shoot as many apples as you can before you run out of time.

The blue bar indicates how much time is left.
Apples:  They will explode when you shoot them.
And some of them will grow back.
Shooting:  Move your head to aim at the apples via the red dot.
Press to shoot.
Every apple you shoot will gain you some time.
You can't shoot at , they are growing back.
