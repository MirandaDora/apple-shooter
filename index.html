About the Game

This is a game designed to showcase xLabs' technology. xLabs captures face pose using a webcam.

The game requires you to move your head so that you can control the cross-hair. This exercise is good for relaxing the shoulder muscles after hours of sitting. xLabs makes it cheap for everyone to play games with their bodies.

I hope you can enjoy the game. If you have any questions or find any bugs, please don't hesitate to contact me.
